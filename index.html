About Me

I am Jismi Jose, a passionate Computer Science graduate. I have completed my bachelor's degree in engineering in 2024. Currently I am upskilling myself through full stack development course. I am interested in learning new technologies which i am not familiar with. I am an energetic person with a positive approach towards surroundings.

During my Bachelor's degree, I undertook several projects that leveraged various technologies. For my miniproject, I developed "MeetMentor," a teacher-student interaction platform, utilizing Figma, Flutter and Firebase. My research experience includes completing a project at IIIT Hyderabad focused on the "Perception and Prosody Analysis" of speech in depressed vs. non-depressed individuals, developed a CNN model and authored a journal paper. Additionally, I built a "Wild Animal Detection System" using Machine Learning, implemented as a functional web application.

Perception and Prosody Analysis

  • Research project analyzing perception and production of prosody in depressed vs. non-depressed individuals.
  • A Convolutional Neural Network (CNNs) model was used for detecting depression from speech.
  • Data collected from participants reading sentences from the Intonational Variation in English (IViE) corpus in both English and Malayalam.
  • Trained the CNN model to identify acoustic features associated with depression in speech, focusing on languages with distinct prosodic and phonemic characteristics.
